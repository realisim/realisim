<h2>november 17th 2015</h2>
<p>This release is available <strong>only on mac</strong> because I am having problems with the deployment on windows. It will be fixed soon.</p>

<p>
-Support for devanagari script.<br>
-Font size can now be changed.<br>
-Enhanced rendering of the notes<br>
<blockquote>
-Octaves (dots) above or under notes are clearer.<br>
-Notes rendering is more natural.<br>
-Fixed multiples rendering issues on the windows version.<br>
</blockquote>
-Fixed numerous small bugs
</p>

<p>
A tutorial is now available on the main page at https://sargamdev.wordpress.com/<br>
